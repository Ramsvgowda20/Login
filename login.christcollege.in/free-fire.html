FREE FIRE


DESCRIPTION
“In Free Fire, there are no limits to what you can accomplish if you put your mind to it.” Free fire is the ultimate game of survival. The classic match has only one round that involves 13 squads.The number of kills could not be counted The last team that survives will be the winner and followed by the runner.The result may be positive or negative. Never forget to give your best.

No. of Rounds : 1
No. of Participants in a Team : 4


RULES

  • Participants must have free fire max installed in their respective devices.
  • Participants must have reached level-20 and above.
  • If the participants are found using any malicious software or hack tools they will be disqualified on the spot.
  • Room ID and password will be given to the participants 10 min prior to the match.
  • Gun skins will be switched off during the match by the host.
