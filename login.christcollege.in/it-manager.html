DESCRIPTION
IT MANAGER is an event which tests your knowledge on solid communication, problem solving, decision making, ability to think and act strategically. Round 1 which is aptitude that will be based on PGCET, Entrance level competitive exams. Round 2, Round 3 and Round 4 will be revealed on the spot.Participants are expected to be equipped with minimal Technical Knowledge.

RULES

  • Category : Individual Event
  • ENTRY FEE:100RS
  • 4 rounds will be conducted
  • Round 1 which is aptitude that will be based on PGCET, Entrance level competitive exams.
  • Round 2, Round 3 and Round 4 will be revealed on the spot.
